Observer Influence in Quantum Gravity

Introduction

The concept of Observer Influence in Quantum Gravity posits that the presence of an observer can significantly alter the behavior of quantum systems. This idea, emerging from the intersection of quantum mechanics and general relativity, suggests a profound connection between observation and the fabric of spacetime.

Observer Influence Equation

The Observer Influence Tensor, represented as \( O_{\mu\nu} = \frac{1}{2}(g_{\mu\nu} + u_{\mu}u_{\nu}) \), mathematically models the impact of observation on quantum states and spacetime.
